Soft warm content card. Compose service tiles, blog cards, pricing, etc.

```jsx
<Card variant="surface" eyebrow="Service" title="Brand Foundation">
  <p>Positioning, identity and messaging that gives every touchpoint a clear, consistent base.</p>
  <Button variant="ghost">Explore →</Button>
</Card>
```

Variants: `surface` (white, shadowed — default), `blush` (rose tint, flat), `inverse` (espresso, for dark sections), `outline`. Pass `media` for a flush-top image. Also exports `Eyebrow` and `Badge` from this group.
